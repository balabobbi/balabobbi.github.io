Wabco Toolbox Plus 14.2.1.0 is a diagnostic software that supports SAE J1939 and J1708 vehicle control systems, as well as PLC products. It allows users to run computer diagnostics, providing detailed information about the vehicle's system, displaying active and stored diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs), offering repair instructions, and enabling system component activation to verify system integrity. It's designed to help technicians troubleshoot and repair vehicle systems efficiently.

The key diagnostic features of the software include:

  • Pneumatic ABS for tractors and trailers: Retrieve both static and dynamic system data, along with active and stored faults. You can also activate system components to ensure proper installation, operation, and system integrity.
  • RSS Plus for trailers: Diagnose driver assistance and stability systems, review fault codes, and activate system components.
  • Hydraulic ABS and hydraulic brakes for tractors: Perform diagnostics on hydraulic brake systems, including activating components, and check related faults and repair instructions.
  • Electronic leveling valves for tractors: Access diagnostics for electronic leveling valves used in tractor suspension and leveling systems.
  • Electronic leveling valves for trailers: Diagnose electronic leveling valves in trailer suspension and leveling systems.
  • Electronically Controlled Air Suspension (ECAS) for buses: Utilize diagnostics for ECAS systems in buses to ensure proper air suspension control.
  • Onguard collision safety systems: Diagnose and configure Onguard collision safety systems for enhanced vehicle safety.

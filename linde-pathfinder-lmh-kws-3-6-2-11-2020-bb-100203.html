Linde Pathfinder LMH + KWS 3.6.2.11 (2020) is a diagnostic software used for Linde warehouse equipment and forklift trucks. It provides tools for guided diagnostics, error codes and parameters, inputs and outputs. The software includes comprehensive documentation such as user guides, maintenance manuals, repair instructions, wiring diagrams, and technical specifications. 

It supports various Linde Material Handling models, including the E, H, P, W, R, T, N, L, V, KA, X_Flash, and Xtra series.

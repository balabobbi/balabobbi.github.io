Detroit Diesel Diagnostic Link (DDDL) 8.20 SP1 is professional diagnostic software for Detroit and Freightliner Cascadia vehicle systems. It allows users to access ECU information, read diagnostic fault codes, run service routines, reprogram engine controllers, perform system tests, and adjust parameters. With DDDL, you can quickly diagnose and fix issues with Detroit vehicles, offering full access to important data and the ability to perform engine calibrations and injector cut-out tests. It’s an all-in-one tool for maintaining and troubleshooting Detroit and Freightliner vehicles efficiently.

DDDL provide:

  • get access to ECU information;
  • read diagnostic fault codes for vehicles;
  • run service routines;
  • reprogram diesel equipment;
  • perform all-system functional and injector cut-out tests;
  • change parameters;
  • perform engine calibration.


Detroit Diesel DDDL with additional features and GRAYED parameters support.

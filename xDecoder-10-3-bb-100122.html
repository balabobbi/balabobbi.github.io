xDecoder 10.3 it`s the program for quickly removing any errors from the ECU firmware. With it, you can disable lambda sensors (Euro 2), EGR, or any other sensor. You can do this yourself without the help of calibrators. The program is tied to the hardware of the PC on which it is installed. Remember! After making changes to the firmware file, it is necessary to calculate the checksum.

Bendix ACom v2 2024 is diagnostic software designed for the diagnosis and maintenance of brake and stability control systems in commercial vehicles. It helps technicians quickly and accurately identify issues, update software, and configure system parameters.

What's new:

  • iSense Pro Continuous Wear Sensing key data added to Bendix DTC Report and Bendix Trailer DTC Report.
  • Driver Assistance Systems (DAS).
  • Updated support for AG12 software version for Steering Assist ECUs.
  • Bi-Directional support for Bendix Driver Assistance Systems:

         - Bi-Directional Test or Calibration Supported on Blindspotter Configuration.

Bendix ACom PRO supported:

  • Antilock Braking (ABS) / Automatic Traction Control (ATC) / Stability Control:

- Electronic Controllers, including the Bendix EC-60, and EC-80 units

  • Collision Mitigation (FLC-20, FLR-20, FLR-21, and more):

- The family of Bendix Wingman driver assistance systems, including Bendix Wingman ACB, Wingman Advanced, and Wingman Fusion

  • Alert & Data Systems:

- VORAD-VS400 and VORAD-VS500 radar systems

- Bendix DIU (driver interface unit)

- SafetyDirect processor (SDP)

- AutoVue Lane Departure Warning Systems (3G and 4G)

- BlindSpotter side object detection

- ADB2ZX Air Disc Brake (ADB) pad wear sensing

Hyundai Microcat EPC contains information on Spare parts and accessories of Hyundai and Genesis vehicles, trucks, buses, pickups, and SUVs of all worldwide markets.

Also includes interactive features that enhance the user experience. Advanced search functionalities, such as VIN or part number searches, enable users to pinpoint parts effortlessly. Furthermore, the platform saves users’ search history, making revisiting previous inquiries and orders easy.

Microcat is an electronic parts and service selling system. Some of its advantages are:

  • Fast and accurate parts interpretation
  • Electronic transfer of parts to your Dealer Management System (DMS)

The Vehicle Index displays pages of digital images for each catalog contained within Microcat. The Vehicle Index is the initial screen displayed when logging onto Microcat. Vehicle catalogs can be loaded using the Vehicle Index; however, Microcat will be limited to only interpreting parts based on the model.

Bendix ACom PRO supported products & components for powered vehicles & trailers.
Diagnostics at your fingertips to quickly troubleshoot, repair, and recalibrate Bendix systems & components.

Bendix ACom PRO Diagnostic Software is a diagnostic tool used in the commercial trucking industry for diagnosing and troubleshooting issues with Bendix anti-lock braking systems (ABS), stability control systems, and other related components. Bendix Commercial Vehicle Systems is a leading manufacturer of safety and braking systems for commercial vehicles.

Heavy-Duty Features:
• Support for vehicle connections using new Noregon DLA+ 3.0, or DLA+ 3.0 Wireless adapter.

• Support for trailer diagnostics using:
- Trailers equipped with 9-Pin SAE J1939 connector using a Noregon DLA+ 3.0, DLA+ 2.0, DLA+, DLA+ 3.0
Wireless, DLA+ 2.0 Wireless, DLA+ Wireless, DLA+ PLC, or Nexiq USB-Link™ 2 adapter.

• Now supports trailer diagnostics using DLA+ 3.0, or DLA+ 3.0 Wireless adapter with:
- Universal J560 PLC Adapter.
- 4-pin to 9-pin extended diagnostic cable adapter.

• Updated key data points window for ADB Continuous Wear Sensing and ABD Wear Sensing ECUs.

• Displays percentage of estimated brake wear life (pads plus rotor) remaining on the Trailer ADB Continuous.

- AutoVue® FLC20™ Camera

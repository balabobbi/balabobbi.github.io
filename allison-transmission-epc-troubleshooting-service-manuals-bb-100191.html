Allison Transmission EPC is a comprehensive resource that includes troubleshooting and service manuals for various Allison transmission models. These manuals cover a range of product families, such as the 1000, 2000, 3000, and 4000 series, and offer detailed guidance on vocational models, operator instructions, parts catalogs, and service procedures. They also include information on Allison's 4th and 5th generation controls, covering motorhomes, off-highway transmissions, and electronic control systems. The manuals provide crucial information for mechanics and operators, enabling them to efficiently manage diagnostics, repairs, and maintenance across various models and transmission configurations.

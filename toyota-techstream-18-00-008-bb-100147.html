Toyota Techstream 18.00.008 is a diagnostic and customization software for use with Toyota, Lexus, and Scion car models. toyota developed techstream for internal use and for customers who want to be a little more hands-on when dealing with their car.

This software allows you to:

  • Diagnose all electronic systems with a digital interface (engine, automatic transmission, VSC, ABS, SRS, and others);
  • Perform various diagnostic procedures and adjustments (including cylinder-by-cylinder fuel injector cut-off, ECB brake calibration, and more);
  • Modify the behavior of various systems (for example: disable the seatbelt warning buzzer, adjust the time required to shift into reverse gear in milliseconds, etc.);
  • Add smart keys (with the presence of special software - Passcode generator program);
  • Program tire pressure sensors for both sets of tires;
  • Flash calibrations for automatic transmission and engine.

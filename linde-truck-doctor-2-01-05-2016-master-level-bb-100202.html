Linde Truck Doctor 2.01.05 (2016) is a diagnostic software designed for Linde trucks and forklifts. It provides essential diagnostic information, including Linde circuit and hydraulic diagrams. This tool helps identify current and potential issues in Linde equipment, allows configuration of products, and provides data for analysis and troubleshooting.

It covers various Linde models, including electric trucks, diesel/LPG trucks, pallet stackers, order pickers, reach trucks, and tractors. Additionally, it includes diagnostics for Linde controllers, combined instruments, dashboards, and accessories.
